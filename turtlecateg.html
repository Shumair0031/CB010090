Turtles

Turtle Categories

The Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery is home to four different species of sea turtles

Hikkaduwa Beach is a popular nesting site for sea turtles, particularly Olive Ridley and Green turtles, among others. Visitors may observe these amazing animals come ashore to lay their eggs during nesting season, which usually occurs between December and April. The Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery works hard to protect these turtles and their nests from threats such as poaching, predators, and habitat degradation, ensuring the survival of future generations.
