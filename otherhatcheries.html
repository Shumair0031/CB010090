Other Hatcheries

More Hatcheries

Apart from the Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery, there are two other notable turtle hatcheries in Sri Lanka:
the Bentota Turtle Hatchery and the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery

The Bentota Turtle Hatchery, located on the island of Bentota, is dedicated to the protection and rehabilitation of Olive Ridley, Green, and Hawksbill turtles. It performs significant research, increases turtle conservation awareness, and provides a safe haven for injured or sick turtles. Similarly, via its nesting programs and educational activities, the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery in the hamlet of Kosgoda, which plays an important role in safeguarding and conserving numerous sea turtle species.
